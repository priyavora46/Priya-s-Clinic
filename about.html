About Our Clinic

Priya's Clinic provides comprehensive pediatric care with a focus on each child's unique needs.

We are committed to fostering a nurturing environment that promotes health and well-being for every child.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional healthcare services that ensure the well-being and happiness of every child we serve. We aim to provide a safe and welcoming atmosphere where families feel valued and empowered to participate in their child's healthcare journey. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement, ensuring that our medical practices and technologies remain at the forefront of pediatric care.

Our Vision

To be the leading pediatric clinic recognized for excellence in healthcare and patient satisfaction. We envision a future where every child has access to high-quality healthcare, and we strive to be a trusted partner for families in our community. Through innovation, education, and advocacy, we aspire to improve children's health outcomes and contribute to the overall well-being of our society.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We care for our patients as if they were our own children.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for the best outcomes in all that we do.
  • Collaboration: We work together with families and healthcare providers to achieve optimal care.
  • Respect: We honor the individuality of each child and family, valuing their unique experiences and perspectives.

Why Choose Us?

At Priya's Clinic, we believe in personalized care tailored to the individual needs of each child. Our dedicated staff is here to ensure that every visit is a positive experience, and we prioritize open communication with parents and caregivers. We are committed to making each appointment as comfortable and informative as possible, empowering families with the knowledge they need to support their child's health.

Our Doctor

Dr. Priya Vora is a qualified child specialist with over 10 years of experience in pediatric healthcare. She is passionate about improving children's health and well-being, and her dedication to her patients is evident in her personalized care approach.

Education

MD in Pediatrics from Marwadi University, 2006

Dr. Vora has pursued extensive training in pediatric medicine, completing her residency at a leading children's hospital where she gained hands-on experience in various pediatric specialties, including neonatology, pediatric cardiology, and developmental-behavioral pediatrics.

Experience

Dr. Vora has worked in various hospitals and clinics, providing care to children of all ages. Throughout her career, she has treated a wide range of conditions, from common illnesses to complex medical issues, ensuring that each child receives the highest quality of care.

In addition to her clinical practice, Dr. Vora has participated in community health programs, educating parents and caregivers on childhood health, nutrition, and preventive care.

Approach to Care

Dr. Vora believes in a family-centered approach, ensuring that both children and parents are involved in the care process. She takes the time to listen to concerns, answer questions, and provide guidance to families, fostering an environment of trust and collaboration.

Her practice emphasizes preventive care, early intervention, and the importance of routine check-ups, vaccinations, and developmental screenings to promote healthy growth and development. Dr. Vora also stays current with the latest advancements in pediatric medicine to provide her patients with the best possible care.
